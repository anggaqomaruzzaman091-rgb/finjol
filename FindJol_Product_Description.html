FindJol: Secure Identity & Fraud Resolution Platform

Version: 1.0 (Release Candidate)
Classification: Enterprise Cybersecurity & Gov-Tech

1. Executive Summary

FindJol is an advanced, offline-first web platform engineered to protect and assist individuals whose identities have been compromised by predatory or fraudulent online loan companies (Pinjol Ilegal). When a job seeker is blacklisted due to identity theft, the bureaucratic process of clearing their name with the Otoritas Jasa Keuangan (OJK) is traditionally slow and complex. FindJol entirely automates this pipeline.

2. Core Target Demographic

  • Victims of Identity Theft: Individuals whose KTP data was stolen and used to secure fraudulent loans.
  • Job Seekers: Professionals actively tracking down negative BI-Checking/SLIK records.
  • Legal Advocates: NGOs or legal aides needing rapid formal complaint generation tools.

3. Application Architecture & Features

Feature: Automated OJK Certificate Generation

Instead of manually drafting formal legal letters, users simply answer a 3-step digital wizard. FindJol captures the chronology of the fraud and instantly binds it to a Print-Ready, Formal OJK Complaint Template. The layout strictly adheres to standard Indonesian bureaucratic guidelines.

Feature: Local PyTorch AI Document Scanning (Zero-Trust)

FindJol includes an enterprise-grade Identity Scanner. Rather than sending highly sensitive KTP photos to external servers (AWS/Google), the backend utilizes local EasyOCR / OpenCV models to execute localized Optical Character Recognition. It actively extracts NIK, Name, Gender, and Address and instantly auto-fills the frontend securely.

4. Technical Stack

  • Frontend: React + TypeScript, Vite, Vanilla CSS.
  • Backend: Python FastAPI, Uvicorn, EasyOCR (PyTorch), OpenCV-Headless.
  • Security: AES-256 Mock Encryption, JWT Architecture, Offline-Capable scanning.
